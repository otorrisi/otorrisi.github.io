Hello,

I am an Assistant Professor of Social Demography in the Department of Sociology at McGill University and a member of McGill's Centre for Population Dynamics. Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Associate in the Social Science Division at New York University Abu Dhabi.

My research focuses on the demography of crises and violence. I am particularly interested in changes in family dynamics in contexts affected by armed violence and disasters, as well as the impact of crises on sexual and reproductive health outcomes.

My work is interdisciplinary. I combine theoretical approaches from demography, sociology, economics and development studies with demographic, causal and spatial methods. I focus largely on low- and middle-income settings.
